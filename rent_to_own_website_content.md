## SECTION 10: FAQ
*StoryBrand element: Address remaining objections and fears*

### Section label
QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

### Headline
**Everything you need to know.**

### Questions to include

**Q: What do I need to qualify?**
Stable income, rental history, and a desire to become a homeowner. Credit scores starting at 580 are considered. If your credit needs work, that's exactly what this program is for — we provide the coaching and tools to get you mortgage-ready.

**Q: How is this different from traditional rent to own?**
We don't inflate home prices, charge premium rent, or require a big upfront option fee. You pay market-rate rent, buy at fair market value, and get $11,000+ in real financial assistance. Traditional rent-to-own locks you in and profits when you fail. We profit when you succeed.

**Q: How does paying rent build my credit?**
Through Esusu, every on-time payment is reported to Equifax, Experian, and TransUnion — building the FICO score mortgage lenders use for qualification. Combined with your Credit Pros action plan, you're building the credit profile lenders want to see.

**Q: What if I'm not ready to buy in 3 years?**
No penalties. You can continue renting, transition to another property, or explore other options. You paid fair market rent — nothing extra is lost.

**Q: How is the purchase price determined?**
Fair market value based on an independent professional appraisal at the time you're ready to buy. You're never locked into an inflated price.

**Q: What about maintenance and repairs?**
Pathway's property management team handles everything at no extra cost while you're renting. Focus on building credit and saving — we handle the house.

**Q: Where is the program available?**
Currently live in Dallas-Fort Worth, Denver, Charlotte, Nashville, and Atlanta. Not all homes in our portfolio qualify — availability depends on the specific property. We also operate traditional rentals in Phoenix, Orlando, and Tampa (Rent to Own coming soon to these markets).

**Q: What is HomeFundIt?**
A crowdfunding platform by CMG Financial where family and friends can contribute to your down payment. Like a wedding registry, but for your first home. CMG also provides $2,000 in closing cost assistance.

**Q: Do I have to use CMG Financial for my mortgage?**
The $2,000 closing cost assistance and HomeFundIt are tied to financing through CMG Financial. You can explore other lenders, but you'd forgo those specific benefits.

### Content principles for the build
- **Homeownership is the mission.** Everything on the page should reinforce that this program exists to create homeowners — not to collect rent.
- **Specificity builds trust.** Use real numbers ($9,000, 3%, $2,000), real partner names, and real timelines. Vague promises sound like every other rent-to-own pitch.
- **Name the villain.** Traditional rent-to-own is the villain. Call out what they do wrong to make Pathway's approach feel like a relief.
- **CTAs everywhere.** Every section should have an exit ramp. The visitor should never have to scroll to find a next step.
- **Fair market value is a feature.** Hammer this point. It's the single biggest differentiator from traditional rent-to-own and it eliminates the biggest fear (overpaying).
